Rules

This is a simple game. You are given 5 words and you have to guess a sixth. Take these for example:

whole - extended - close - large - nuclear

Here the win word would be...family. In fact it is the word which is somehow related to all the 5 given words.

it suits the whole family

mine is an extended family

they really were a close family

my husband's family is a large one...

the idea that the nuclear family is...

Auto-mode

In the auto-mode the tuples of the 5 + 1 (clue and unknown) words are generated automatically from collocations data downloaded from collocates.info.

Current constraints are:

  • the tuple with the 5 words are chosen in order of Mutual Information
  • unknown word (to be guessed) should be a noun
  • clue words should be a noun, a verb, an adjective or a preposition
  • no more than 2 nouns among the 5 clue words
  • no more than 2 verbs among the 5 clue words
  • no more than 2 adjectives among the 5 clue words
  • no more than 1 preposition among the 5 clue words

Got it? Try it!
